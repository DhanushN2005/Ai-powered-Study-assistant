# Demo Video Guide

**Duration**: ~2-3 Minutes

### 1. Introduction & Login (0:00 - 0:30)
- **Visual**: Start on the Landing/Login Page.
- **Action**: Enter credentials for a "Student" account.
- **Narration**: "Welcome to the AI Study Assistant. Today I'll log in as a student to demonstrate how we use AI to enhance learning."

### 2. Dashboard Overview (0:30 - 0:45)
- **Visual**: Dashboard loads with vibrant stat cards and "Good Morning/Evening" greeting.
- **Action**: Hover over the Stat Cards (observe the scale/shadow effects). Scroll down to see "Performance by Subject".
- **Narration**: "Here is the Dashboard. It gives me a quick overview of my study time, streaks, and subject performance. Notice the clean, dark-mode compatible UI."

### 3. Material Upload & AI Summary (0:45 - 1:15)
- **Visual**: Navigate to "Materials".
- **Action**: 
    1. Click "Upload Material".
    2. Upload a sample PDF or Text file.
    3. Once uploaded, click the **"View Summary"** (or "Summarize") button.
    4. Show the modal with the AI-generated summary.
- **Narration**: "I can upload my course materials here. The AI automatically analyzes the content and acts as a tutor. Let's look at the summary it generated for this Physics chapter."

### 4. Quiz Generation (1:15 - 1:50)
- **Visual**: Still on "Materials" page.
- **Action**:
    1. Click **"Generate Quiz"** on a material card.
    2. Select settings (e.g., Medium difficulty, 5 Questions).
    3. Click "Generate".
    4. Transition to the Quiz page and answer a few questions.
    5. Submit and show the "Results" page with the confetti animation.
- **Narration**: "Now, I'll test my knowledge. The AI creates a custom quiz based specifically on that material. After submitting, I get instant feedback and a score."

### 5. Leaderboard & Responsiveness (1:50 - 2:10)
- **Visual**: Navigate to "Leaderboard".
- **Action**:
    1. Show the Top 3 Podium (observe the Crown icon and correct layout).
    2. **Vital**: Resize the browser window to mobile width (simulating a phone) to show that the layout adjusts and nothing clips.
- **Narration**: "The app adds a gamification element with this Leaderboard. It works perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices."

### 6. Discussions (2:10 - 2:30)
- **Visual**: Navigate to "Discussions".
- **Action**: Click creating a new discussion or viewing an existing one. Show the layout.
- **Narration**: "Finally, students can collaborate here. The layout is optimized for readability."
